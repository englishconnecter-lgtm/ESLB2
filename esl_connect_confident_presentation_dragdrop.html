4. Grammar focus: imperatives for advice

Choose the best word to complete the presentation advice.

Grammar reminder

Use the base verb for advice: “Stand tall.”
Use “Don’t + verb” for negative advice.
Imperatives are common in tips and instructions.
Answers: 1 keep, 2 don’t speak, 3 don’t look, 4 practise.

5. Grammar practice: giving recommendations

Complete the sentences with your own ideas. Use should, could, try to or avoid.

Example answers:
1. Before a presentation, you should practise your opening.
2. If you feel nervous, you could pause and breathe slowly.
3. During a presentation, try to make eye contact.
4. When speaking to an audience, avoid reading every word from your notes.

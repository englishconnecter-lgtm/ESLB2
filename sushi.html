5. Grammar practice: explaining change

Complete the sentences with your own ideas. Use words like because, over time, as a result and whereas.

Example answers:
1. Sushi became popular internationally because it was seen as fresh, stylish and different.
2. In the past, sushi was mainly a preservation method, whereas today it is often a restaurant food.
3. When food travels to a new country, it often changes to suit local tastes.
4. Traditional food can change over time as a result of trade, migration and global trends.

6. Thought-provoking discussion

There is not only one correct answer. Try to explain both sides before giving your opinion.

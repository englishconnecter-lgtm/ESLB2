4. Grammar focus: passives for data and research

Choose the best passive form.

Grammar reminder

Present passive: is/are + past participle
Past passive: was/were + past participle
Use the passive when the action is more important than the person doing it.
Answers: 1 is shown, 2 was collected, 3 are understood, 4 are used.
